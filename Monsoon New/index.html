A little something about us !

Monsoon Hotels & Resorts is a privately held company active in hospitality property development and stands for quality and excellence in its delivery. We have the capacity and experience to develop hotels and resorts that strictly adhere to the standards and market demands. We offer great investment opportunities with a fairly short investment return period to potential investors by developing turnkey income producing hotels and resorts and offering them for operations and management by globally well-known hospitality brands. To strengthen profitability, we target major international hospitality brands and utilize their customer base as our launch pad.

During the developing we make sure that we deliver and preserve the legacy, the touch, and expectations of the brand for which we develop the property. While on a much higher level the property development is just the tip of the iceberg. The development includes strategic analysis, development and management auditing, and continuous market surveillance.


The result of this approach, is that we also succeed to meet real estate owners and equity investors their expectations by creating a stable income producing asset. Why work with us ?

End-to-End assistance

No doubt development of a hospitality industry asset is a complicated process. It requires fine fine tuning all the elements involved. The developer must be in control of the whole process from the start to the end, and this involves communication and interactions at different levels in different capacities with contractors, leasing agents, government institutions, legal officers, banks, architects, designers, construction company etc. This process can get very intimidating but we Monsoon Hotels and Resorts will make this entire process very easy for you.

Invest in Turnkey projects

Hospitality industry in the Maldives is the most profitable and thriving industry. Any investment in this sector yields a steady and a guaranteed return on investment. One of the easiest entry into this sector is to develop a property and sub-lease it's management as a turnkey project to a globally renowned hotelier brand or instead manage by the developer itself. We provide you with all the neccessary expertise to support your decision whatever it may be.

Invest in Maldives

Luxury holiday getaway is almost synonymous with the Maldives. Today Maldives is no longer just a country's name, rather it is a brand image that signifies luxury with the utmost highest quality of hospitality services available across the globe. Maldives always ranks at the top of the most desired travel destinations. The Maldives brand image itself attracts a huge global audience. An investment in this sector needs no struggle to market, rather creating and marketing a brand image of the hotel itself is enough and the destination is itself marketed already.

It's all about these few steps

A quick skim Analyze the preliminary possibilities on the market from a hospitality point of view, if we sense potentiality of the location then we proceed to the next stage.
Concept development If it makes through the first stage, then comes the development of the concept. The concept is then benchmarked against the market demand and then a draft of operational profit and loss statement is devised. This is followed by an allocation of a possible master budget for the entire development project.
Letter of Intent Concept development leads to a letter of intent, which contains all the terms and conditions under which a final lease agreement can be drafted.
Finalizing the deal Once all the details have been worked out, the final negotiations are sealed in a final binding lease agreement. Every detail of the final deal is perfectly and transparently worked out by all stakeholders.
